This comic panel is from the eleventh issue of Watchmen titled “Look on My Works, Ye Mighty…” by Alan Moore (who also wrote V for Vendetta) and Dave Gibbons.

This scene depicts one of the most iconic moments in the series, featuring a conversation between Ozymandias and Nite Owl (Dan Dreiberg) alongside Rorschach.

In the scene depicted in this image, Nite Owl tells Ozymandias to execute his plan. Ozymandias responds with the line in the panel, highlighting his strategic foresight by revealing that he had already initiated the plan thirty-five minutes ago. This revelation underscores the futility of their attempt to stop him and the inevitability of his actions.

This moment illustrates Ozymandias' cold, calculated nature and his belief that the end justifies the means, as well as his detachment from the moral and ethical implications of his actions.

The image from Japanese parliament that has become a meme involves Shinichi Yokoyama, the Chairman of the Judicial Affairs Committee in 2018, who found himself at the center of a chaotic scene while trying to push forward a controversial immigration reform bill.

The photograph captures a moment on December 8, 2018, when Yokoyama attempted to initiate a vote on the bill, which aimed to allow foreign workers a path to Japanese citizenship. This proposal, introduced by then-Prime Minister Shinzo Abe, faced fierce opposition from both Japan’s opposition parties and certain members of Abe's own conservative base.

In the image, Yokoyama is holding a microphone, attempting to speak as members of parliament swarm around him in an attempt to restrain him, presumably to prevent the vote from proceeding. This intense moment visually portrays the struggle to control the narrative or halt the legislative process, and it embodies a high-stakes power struggle within a government setting. Notably, physical altercations are not entirely unusual in Japanese parliament; similar scenes occurred in 2015 and 2023.
