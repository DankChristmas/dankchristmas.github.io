Gloved hand flicking over domino from V for Vendetta The domino flick depicts a key scene where V sets off a chain reaction by flicking the first of many dominoes. This moment is symbolic of the broader plan V has put into motion to incite rebellion and bring down the oppressive government. The domino effect represents how a single action can lead to a series of events that ultimately achieve a larger goal. The visual metaphor highlights the impact of V's meticulously planned actions and their far-reaching consequences.
